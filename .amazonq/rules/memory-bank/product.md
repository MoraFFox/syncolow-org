# Product Overview

## Project Purpose
SynergyFlow ERP is an AI-powered enterprise resource planning system designed to streamline business operations through intelligent automation and comprehensive management capabilities.

## Value Proposition
- Unified platform for managing clients, sales, orders, maintenance, and analytics
- AI-driven insights using Google Genkit for intelligent decision-making
- Real-time business intelligence with payment tracking and performance scoring
- Firebase-backed cloud infrastructure for scalability and reliability

## Key Features

### Core Business Management
- **Client Management**: Complete customer relationship tracking with payment history and performance metrics
- **Order Processing**: Full order lifecycle management with CSV import, status tracking, and automated workflows
- **Product Catalog**: Comprehensive product management with manufacturer tracking
- **Maintenance Scheduling**: Service appointment management with barista assignment and visit tracking

### Analytics & Intelligence
- **Payment Analytics**: Client payment scoring, warning systems, and financial health monitoring
- **Performance Dashboards**: Real-time KPIs, revenue tracking, and business metrics visualization
- **Sentiment Analysis**: Customer feedback analysis and satisfaction tracking
- **Advanced Search**: Fuzzy search with auto-tagging and intelligent filtering

### AI-Powered Features
- **Genkit AI Flows**: Automated business logic and intelligent recommendations
- **Auto-Status Updates**: Smart order status transitions based on business rules
- **Notification Generation**: Intelligent alert system for critical business events
- **Predictive Scoring**: Payment risk assessment and performance predictions

### User Experience
- **Responsive Design**: Mobile-first interface with dark/light theme support
- **Real-time Updates**: Live data synchronization across all modules
- **Export Capabilities**: PDF invoice generation and data export utilities
- **Interactive Maps**: Leaflet-based geolocation for client and service tracking

## Target Users
- Small to medium-sized businesses requiring integrated ERP solutions
- Service-based companies managing maintenance and client visits
- Sales teams needing order and client management tools
- Business managers requiring analytics and performance insights

## Use Cases
- Track client orders from creation to delivery with automated status updates
- Monitor payment health and receive early warnings for at-risk accounts
- Schedule and manage maintenance visits with barista assignment
- Generate comprehensive business reports and analytics dashboards
- Import bulk orders via CSV with intelligent data validation
- Analyze customer sentiment and feedback trends
- Export invoices and financial documents in PDF format
